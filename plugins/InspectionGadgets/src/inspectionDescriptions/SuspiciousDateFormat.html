Reports likely suspicious date format patterns. Usages of the following patters are reported:
  • Upper-cased "Y", unless "w" appears nearby because it's a "Week year" that almost always the same as normal "Year" (lower-cased "y" pattern), but may point to the next year at the end of December.
  • Upper-cased "M" (month) close to "H", "K", "h", or "k" (hour). It's likely that lower-cased "m" (minute) was intended
  • Lower-cased "m" (minute) close to "y" (year) or "d" (day in month). It's likely that upper-cased "M" (month) was intended
  • Upper-cased "D" (day in year) close to "M", or "L" (month). It's likely that lower-cased "d" (day in month) was intended
  • Upper-cased "S" (milliseconds) close to "m" (minutes). It's likely that lower-cased "s" (seconds) was intended

Examples:
new SimpleDateFormat("YYYY-MM-dd"): likely "yyyy-MM-dd" was intended.
new SimpleDateFormat("yyyy-MM-DD"): likely "yyyy-MM-dd" was intended.
new SimpleDateFormat("HH:MM"): likely "HH:mm" was intended.
